# Salsa Rayo - Blog Content Plan 2026

## Business Context

- **School:** Salsa Rayo - New York Style Salsa (On2) & Bachata
- **Location:** Agios Dimitrios, Athens
- **Goal:** Attract adults in Athens searching for salsa/bachata classes via organic search + AI citation
- **Differentiator:** Only school in Athens specifically teaching NY Style On2
- **Key insight:** Greek-language dance content is a goldmine - almost no competition for informational queries

---

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Salsa for Beginners (Direct Conversion)
Targets people actively looking to start salsa. Highest ROI.

### Pillar 2: Bachata for Beginners (Growing Demand)
Bachata search volume is rising fast. Less competition than salsa.

### Pillar 3: Health, Fitness & Lifestyle (Top-of-Funnel)
Attracts people not yet searching for dance classes but open to it.

### Pillar 4: Dance Style Education (Authority + Differentiator)
Positions Salsa Rayo as the technical authority. Owns the "On2" niche.

### Pillar 5: Athens Dance Scene & Community (Local SEO)
Captures social dancers and people exploring the Athens latin scene.

## AEO Optimization Notes

Every post should include at least ONE of:
- **Comparison table** (X vs Y) - AI systems love structured comparisons
- **Numbered list** with specific facts - easy to extract as citations
- **FAQ-style H2s** that mirror "People Also Ask" queries
- **Specific numbers** (calories, weeks, prices, percentages)

## Content KPIs

Track monthly:
- Organic impressions per post (Google Search Console)
- Click-through rate per post
- Blog-to-pricing-page conversion (via internal link clicks)
- AI citation appearances (search for brand in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI)
