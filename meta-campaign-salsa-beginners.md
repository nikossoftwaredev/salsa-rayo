## Reel Content Plan (15-30 seconds)

### Concept: "From Zero to Dancing in One Class"

The reel should feel **native and energetic** - not polished/corporate. Film it on a phone, in your actual studio, with real students if possible. **No talking - music only with text overlays in English.**

### Script & Shot Breakdown

| Time | Shot | What's Happening | Text Overlay |
|------|------|------------------|--------------|
| 0-1s | HOOK | Close-up of feet doing a basic salsa step, camera tilts up fast | **"Never danced salsa before?"** |
| 1-3s | Pattern interrupt | Instructor smiles at camera, beckons with hand. Energy, confidence. | **"Let's change that"** |
| 3-6s | Problem | Quick cuts: person sitting alone scrolling phone, looking bored at home/party | **"Tired of the same routine?"** |
| 6-10s | Transformation | Energy shift - show 2-3 beginners laughing while learning basic steps, instructor guiding them patiently | **"One class is all it takes"** |
| 10-18s | Social proof | Show class atmosphere: people dancing together, smiling, high-fiving. Mix of ages/genders. Show couples AND solo people dancing. | **"Learn New York Style Salsa in Athens"** |
| 18-23s | The offer | Bold text overlay on screen with class footage in background | **"First class FREE - No partner needed"** |
| 23-27s | CTA | Quick flash of happy students dancing, then end card with logo | **"Sign up NOW - Limited spots"** |

### Music

- Use an energetic salsa track from Meta's licensed music library
- Match the beat drops to transitions (especially the 3s and 6s energy shift)
- Keep the vibe fun and inviting, not intimidating

### Key Creative Rules

1. **First 1-3 seconds are everything** - Use movement, close-ups, or a bold question to stop the scroll
2. **NO TALKING** - Music + text overlays only. Works for all languages and sound-off viewers
3. **Text overlays: big, bold, easy to read** - White text with dark shadow/outline, centered
4. **Show real people, not stock** - Authenticity beats production value
5. **Film VERTICAL (9:16)** - This is for Reels
6. **Show solo people having fun** - The #1 objection is "I don't have a partner"
7. **Include diverse ages** - You're targeting 18-45, show that range
8. **Show non-Greek faces too** - If you have international students, include them to attract expats

### Alternative Reel Angles to A/B Test

| B | "3 things you learn in your first salsa class" | Educational/curiosity |
| C | Before/after of a real student (first day vs. 1 month later) | Social proof |

## Lead Form Setup (Instant Form)

### Form Type: "More Volume" (not "Higher Intent")

Since you want maximum leads, use the simpler form. Meta pre-fills most fields = less friction = more submissions.

### Form Structure

**Hero Section:**
- Image: Your best class photo (people dancing, smiling)
- Headline: **"Free Trial Salsa Class"**
- Description: **"Take your first step into salsa - no experience, no partner needed. Limited spots available."**

**Questions (keep it to 3-4 MAX for volume):**

| # | Question | Type | Why |
|---|----------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Full Name** | Pre-filled | Essential for follow-up |
| 2 | **Email** | Pre-filled | For email follow-up sequence |
| 3 | **Phone** | Pre-filled | Critical - Phone follow-up converts 3-5x better than email for local businesses |
| 4 | **"Have you danced salsa before?"** | Multiple choice: No, never / Yes, a little / Yes, a lot | Qualifies the lead AND helps you personalize follow-up |

**That's it. 4 questions. Don't add more.** Every extra question drops conversion rate 5-10%. You can ask everything else when they show up or when you call them.

### Thank You Screen

- **Headline:** "We can't wait to see you!"
- **Description:** "We'll contact you within 24 hours to book your free class."
- **CTA Button:** "Visit our website" -> link to your website
- **Optional:** Add your phone number and Instagram link

## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Speed to Contact

**Call leads within 5 minutes of submission.** This is the #1 factor for local service lead conversion. After 30 minutes, conversion drops 80%. Set up:

- Meta lead notification on your phone
- Or connect to a CRM (even a Google Sheet via Zapier works)

### 2. Follow-Up Sequence

| Timing | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 0-5 min | Phone call |
| If no answer | WhatsApp message |
| 1 hour later | Second call attempt |
| 24 hours | Follow-up WhatsApp with class schedule |
| 3 days | Final follow-up with urgency ("2 remaining spots this week") |

### 3. Track Everything

- Set up UTM parameters on your website link
- Track: Leads submitted -> Contacted -> Showed up -> Enrolled
- Your target: 30-40% of leads should show up for trial class

### 4. Budget Scaling Rules

- Don't touch anything for the first 3-5 days (learning phase)
- If CPA (cost per lead) is good, increase budget by 20% every 3-4 days
- If CPA is too high after 1,000+ impressions, change the creative first
- Expected CPA for Athens dance classes: **2-6 EUR per lead**
