## Why Learn Salsa?

Salsa is more than just a dance  - it's a way of life. Born from the vibrant rhythms of Cuba and the Caribbean, salsa has become one of the most popular social dances in the world, and for good reason. Whether you're looking for a new hobby, a way to stay fit, or a gateway into a thriving social community, learning salsa opens doors you never knew existed.

**Social connection** is at the heart of salsa. Unlike solo activities, partner dancing creates an instant bond between two people on the dance floor. You'll meet new friends, build confidence in social settings, and become part of a warm, welcoming community that spans the globe. Salsa nights happen in every major city, meaning you'll never feel like a stranger wherever you travel.

**Fitness and well-being** come naturally when you dance. A single hour of salsa burns between 400 and 600 calories, improves cardiovascular health, strengthens your core, and enhances coordination  - all without the monotony of a gym routine. You'll be having so much fun that you won't even realize you're working out.

**Cultural enrichment** is another beautiful side effect. Through salsa, you'll discover Afro-Cuban music, learn about the history of son, timba, and casino, and develop an ear for clave rhythms that have shaped popular music worldwide.

Above all, salsa is **pure fun**. There's nothing quite like the feeling of moving in sync with a partner to a great song, nailing a combination you've been practicing, or simply letting loose on the dance floor after a long day.

## Our Salsa Classes

At Salsa Rayo, we teach **New York Style Salsa (On2)**  - the smooth, musical style of salsa that emerged from the legendary dance scene of New York City. NY-style salsa is elegant, expressive, and deeply connected to the music, making it the perfect style for dancers who value musicality and precision.

We offer **Salsa, Mambo, Pachanga, Afro-Fusion, and Boogaloo**. Each style is offered at multiple levels so you can progress at your own pace.

### Beginners

No experience required. In our beginner classes, you'll learn the basic step and timing, fundamental turns and combinations, partner work basics, and musicality fundamentals. Our beginners course is designed to get you comfortable on the social dance floor within weeks. Classes are structured, progressive, and always supportive  - no judgment, just progress.

### Improvers

Once you've built a solid foundation, our improvers classes help you bridge the gap to intermediate dancing. You'll expand your vocabulary with more combinations and smoother transitions, develop your musicality, strengthen your partner connection, and build confidence for social dancing at a higher level.

### Intermediate

Our intermediate classes are for dancers ready to take their skills to the next level. You'll work on complex turn patterns and combinations, advanced musicality, improvisation skills, and overall performance quality  - polishing your execution, timing, and presence on the dance floor.

### Mambo & Pachanga

An intermediate-level class focused on footwork and musicality. Mambo and Pachanga sharpen your technique, build confidence on the dance floor, and deepen your connection to the music.

### Ladies Styling

A fundamentals class for followers covering basic arm styling and body movement  - the building blocks of expressive dancing.

### Movement & Styling

An intermediate-level class that takes styling further with more complex combinations, deeper body movement, and exploration of salsa fusion elements.

### Salsa Techniques

A foundations class where you learn from scratch how to move your body in the basic steps  - like son or swing. Perfect for building proper technique and body awareness from the ground up.

### Salsa Power Up

Open to all levels  - from students to professional dancers. This class builds endurance and stamina for dancing, improves mind-to-muscle connection for better body movement, and doubles as a workout. Great for anyone who wants to dance longer, move better, and train harder.
