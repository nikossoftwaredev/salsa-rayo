## Why Learn Salsa?

Salsa is more than just a dance — it's a way of life. Born from the vibrant rhythms of Cuba and the Caribbean, salsa has become one of the most popular social dances in the world, and for good reason. Whether you're looking for a new hobby, a way to stay fit, or a gateway into a thriving social community, learning salsa opens doors you never knew existed.

**Social connection** is at the heart of salsa. Unlike solo activities, partner dancing creates an instant bond between two people on the dance floor. You'll meet new friends, build confidence in social settings, and become part of a warm, welcoming community that spans the globe. Salsa nights happen in every major city, meaning you'll never feel like a stranger wherever you travel.

**Fitness and well-being** come naturally when you dance. A single hour of salsa burns between 400 and 600 calories, improves cardiovascular health, strengthens your core, and enhances coordination — all without the monotony of a gym routine. You'll be having so much fun that you won't even realize you're working out.

**Cultural enrichment** is another beautiful side effect. Through salsa, you'll discover Afro-Cuban music, learn about the history of son, timba, and casino, and develop an ear for clave rhythms that have shaped popular music worldwide.

Above all, salsa is **pure fun**. There's nothing quite like the feeling of moving in sync with a partner to a great song, nailing a combination you've been practicing, or simply letting loose on the dance floor after a long day.

## Our Salsa Classes

At Salsa Rayo, we teach **Cuban Salsa (Casino)** — the original, circular style of salsa that emerged from the streets and social clubs of Havana. Cuban salsa is playful, grounded, and deeply connected to the music, making it the perfect style for social dancing.

### Level 1 — Beginners

No experience required. In our beginner classes, you'll learn:

- **The basic step and timing** — understanding the rhythm, stepping on the beat, and building a solid foundation
- **Fundamental turns and combinations** — enchufla, dile que no, and other essential moves
- **Partner work basics** — leading, following, connection, and frame
- **Musicality fundamentals** — recognizing the clave, the one-count, and basic song structure

Our beginners course is designed to get you comfortable on the social dance floor within weeks. Classes are structured, progressive, and always supportive — no judgment, just progress.

### Level 2 — Intermediate

Ready to level up? Our intermediate classes build on your foundation with:

- **Complex combinations and transitions** — longer sequences, smoother execution, and creative variations
- **Advanced musicality** — dancing to breaks, accents, and different instruments in the music
- **Rueda de Casino** — the iconic Cuban group dance where couples form a circle and execute synchronized moves
- **Improvisation skills** — learning to freestyle with confidence and express your personality through movement

### Mambo — Advanced Technique

For dancers who want to refine their craft, our Mambo sessions focus on **footwork, body isolation, and rhythmic precision**. This is where technique meets artistry, helping you stand out on any dance floor.

### Ladies Styling

A dedicated class for followers who want to develop their own expressive movement. Learn **arm styling, body waves, hip movement, and turns** that add flair and personality to your dancing while maintaining strong partner connection.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need any prior dance experience to join?**

Not at all. Our Level 1 classes are designed specifically for complete beginners. We start from the very basics — how to step, how to count the music, and how to connect with a partner. Many of our most passionate dancers walked in with zero experience.

**Do I need to bring a partner?**

No. You're welcome to come alone, with a friend, or with a partner. In class, we rotate partners regularly so that everyone gets to practice with different people. This is actually one of the best ways to improve quickly, as you learn to adapt to different leading and following styles.

**How much do classes cost?**

Our plans start at just €50 per month for 8 classes. If you want to dance more frequently, our 16-class plan is €75/month and our 24-class plan is €99/month. There are no sign-up fees or long-term contracts — just straightforward monthly pricing.

**What should I wear to class?**

Wear comfortable clothes that allow you to move freely. For shoes, smooth-soled shoes work best — avoid rubber soles that grip the floor. Many dancers start with regular sneakers and later invest in dedicated dance shoes as they progress. Most importantly, bring water and a positive attitude!
