## Our Story

Every great dance school has an origin story, and ours begins the way the best ones do  - on the dance floor.

**Konstantinos Bitsis** and **Anna Lontou** met in 2018 as teenagers who shared one overwhelming passion: Latin dance. What started as a casual dance partnership quickly became something deeper. They discovered a rare chemistry  - the kind that makes an audience stop and watch, the kind that judges notice.

From that point on, they were inseparable. Not just as dance partners, but as creative collaborators with a shared vision. Together, they traveled across Europe and beyond  - from salsa congresses in Spain and Germany to festivals in Italy, and even across the Atlantic to the legendary New York salsa scene. Every trip was a chance to train with the best, absorb new styles, and push their own boundaries.

Along the way, they competed in salsa competitions at both the duet and group level, **winning awards** that validated years of relentless practice. But for Konstantinos and Anna, the trophies were never the point. What drove them was the desire to share what they'd learned  - to bring that world-class energy back home to Greece.

In **2025**, that dream became a reality. They founded **Salsa Rayo** in Agios Dimitrios, Athens  - a dance school built on the belief that everyone deserves access to high-quality Latin dance instruction in a warm, welcoming environment.

The name **"Rayo"** means *lightning* in Spanish. It represents everything the school stands for: **strength, speed, energy**, and the electric spark that ignites when music meets movement. When you step onto our floor, you feel it.

## Meet Konstantinos Bitsis  - Senior Instructor

Konstantinos is the technical backbone of Salsa Rayo. Known for his **precision, musicality, and ability to break down complex patterns** into digestible steps, he has a teaching style that makes even the most intimidating combinations feel achievable.

His journey in Latin dance began in his early teens, and he quickly stood out for his dedication. While other dancers were learning the basics, Konstantinos was already seeking out advanced workshops, studying under **world-renowned instructors** from Cuba, Colombia, and the United States. That global training gave him a deep understanding of multiple salsa styles, with New York Style (On2) becoming his specialty and first love.

On the competition circuit, Konstantinos earned recognition for his sharp timing, dynamic energy, and ability to lead with clarity and confidence. But ask any of his students, and they'll tell you his greatest strength isn't his own dancing  - it's his ability to **make you a better dancer**. He has a gift for identifying what each student needs, adjusting his approach, and building their confidence step by step.

His teaching philosophy is straightforward: **technique is the foundation, but feeling is the goal**. Every pattern, every turn, every body movement serves the music. Once you understand that, you stop counting steps and start dancing.

## Meet Anna Lontou  - Lead Instructor

Anna has been dancing since she was **six years old**. Before she ever stepped into a salsa class, she had already spent years training in other dance forms, giving her a foundation of body awareness and musicality that few instructors can match.

When she discovered Latin dance as a teenager, everything clicked. She found her home in salsa and bachata  - drawn to the improvisation, the partner connection, and the freedom of expression that social dancing offers. She dove in headfirst, training intensively and developing a style that blends **technical precision with artistic creativity**.

Anna specializes in **styling, body movement, and feminine expression**  - the elements that transform good dancing into captivating dancing. Her classes don't just teach you steps; they teach you how to move with intention, how to use your arms and body to complement the music, and how to develop your own personal style.

Beyond technique, Anna brings an infectious **passion and warmth** to every class. She creates an atmosphere where students feel safe to experiment, make mistakes, and grow. Her energy is contagious  - walk into one of her classes feeling tired after work, and within minutes you'll be fully immersed in the music, forgetting everything else.

Together, Konstantinos and Anna form a teaching duo that covers every aspect of Latin dance  - from the technical precision of leading and following to the artistic expression that makes each dancer unique.

## Our Teaching Philosophy

At Salsa Rayo, we believe that great dancing is built on five pillars:

### Structured Progression

We offer clearly defined levels so you always know where you are and what's next. **Complete beginners are always welcome**  - our introductory programs are designed for people who have never danced a step in their lives. From there, you progress through levels at your own pace, building skills methodically.

### Technique First, Always

Flashy moves mean nothing without a solid foundation. We emphasize **proper technique** from day one  - posture, timing, weight transfer, lead-follow connection. These fundamentals make everything else possible and ensure you can dance comfortably with any partner, anywhere in the world.

### Fun Is Non-Negotiable

Yes, we take technique seriously. But this isn't a military academy  - it's a dance school. Our classes are filled with laughter, music, and genuine enjoyment. We believe you learn faster when you're having fun, and we design every class to balance focused practice with pure dancing joy.

### No Partner? No Problem

We use **partner rotation** in every class. This isn't just a logistical choice  - it's a pedagogical one. Dancing with multiple partners makes you a stronger, more adaptable dancer. It also means you never need to bring someone with you. Come alone, and you'll dance with everyone.

### Community Over Competition

Salsa Rayo isn't just a school  - it's a **community**. Our students go out dancing together, attend festivals together, and support each other's growth. We organize regular social nights, workshops with guest instructors, and group trips to salsa events. When you join Salsa Rayo, you're not signing up for a class  - you're joining a family.
